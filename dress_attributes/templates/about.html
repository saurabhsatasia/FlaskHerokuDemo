About Project

  • 2 types of Datasets: Contains dress attributes and recommendations according to the proceeds of the sale.
  • Sales are monitored based on alternative days. This data set consists of 500 data and 13 variables. Now, classification is done based on the recommendation variable.
  • Attributes Dataset Features: Style, Price, Rating, Size, Season, NeckLine, SleeveLength, waiseline, Material, FabricType, Decoration, Pattern, Type, Recommendation are Attributes in dataset.
  • Sales Dataset: Sales are monitor on the basis of alternate days.
  • Problem Statement: To build a classification model based on the given features in both the datasets i.e. Dress Attributes and their respective sales data.
